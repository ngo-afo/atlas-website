Prior to founding Atlas Financial Operations, Stephen Grofcsik served as Operations Manager at Virage Capital Management LP, where he oversaw a $400 million private equity fund in the litigation finance space. The fund's unique strategy required a multi-disciplinary approach, integrating expertise in finance, law, and information technology. Mr. Grofcsik served as the primary liaison for the fund's audit, accounting, tax, and valuation functions. His role also encompassed managing the day-to-day operations of the fund such as financial reporting, investor communications, treasury operations, and portfolio monitoring. In addition to overseeing day-to-day operations, his responsibilities extended to supporting key initiatives such as fundraising, transaction structuring, and due diligence.

Prior to Virage, Mr. Grofcsik was a Finance Director for TFI International, a Canadian based transportation and logistics company operating across the United States, Canada and Mexico through its subsidiaries. In this role, Mr. Grofcsik provided operational oversight and support to several independent operating divisions representing roughly $1 billion in annual revenue. He worked with the division management teams to achieve budget performance, improve profitability, realize cost synergies, and implement strategic plans to grow the business. In addition, Mr. Grofcsik performed due diligence and valuation for several potential acquisitions.

Prior to joining TForce Final Mile, Mr. Grofcsik worked at Rothstein Kass & Company, PLLC, where he supervised audits for long/short equity funds, commodity derivative funds, fund of funds, and managed accounts. His responsibilities included financial statement preparation and review, partnership tax return preparation, and training personnel. Prior to joining Rothstein Kass & Company, Mr. Grofcsik worked for Netherland, Sewell, & Associates where he supported oil and gas reserve evaluation engagements and prepared SEC reserve reports for large oil and gas clients.

Mr. Grofcsik received his Bachelors of Business Administration and Master in Professional Accounting from The University of Texas at Austin in 2003 and became a Certified Public Accountant in 2011.
